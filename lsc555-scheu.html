Things I never expected to do as a Library Director

by Cindy Scheu

Library Director

There are many things I expected to do as a Library Director, but so far there are many more things I never expected to have to do. Small, rural public libraries offer you the chance to be a part of the community and sometimes that means as a librarian you end up doing things you never expected to have to do, such as:

  • Knowing how to trim a Japanese Maple
  • Painting a bathroom
  • Having to file a police report for theft

Even with the strange and sometimes off the wall things I have to do, I still love being a librarian.
